Single-clip overfit (S2 · cell D): anchor gap 0.071 m, no micro-stepping, eyeball-verified ~70–80% fidelity.

2 · Why it matters

This is the ceiling proof for the whole project: SONIC's stack can reproduce reference motion on the X2 embodiment to near-reference fidelity. Every diagnosis since anchors on it:

  • Anything the overfit policy can do that the fleet policy can't is a training problem — never blamed on the robot.
  • The residual ~20–30% (slightly different flavor, no micro-steps) is consistent with the three embodiment boundaries (shoulder, leg length, motor speed — see the boundaries report).
